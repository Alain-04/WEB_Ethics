About Our Company

NEURA-STRUCT Engineering Technologies started operations in 2018, focusing on developing monitoring systems that enable building and infrastructure safety assessments through advanced engineering solutions and artificial intelligence technology. We unite traditional engineering approaches with contemporary digital monitoring systems including sensors and data processing capabilities.

Our Mission

NEURA-STRUCT is dedicated to identifying structural issues at their onset, serving property owners, engineering firms, construction companies, and municipalities across Lebanon and the Middle East. We combine traditional engineering expertise with IoT sensors and AI-based evaluation to deliver structural monitoring systems, sensor deployment services, data evaluation solutions, and warning reports. Our commitment to enduring growth through service excellence and strategic alliances ensures financial stability, while our philosophy centers on integrity, responsibility, transparency, respect, and sustainability. We achieve competitive advantage through precise data analysis and quick response capabilities, working to create safer cities while promoting environmental stewardship. Our employees are vital assets, receiving continuous development through safety training and ethical education to maintain the highest standards of responsibility and respect.

Ethical Code

Our Ethical Code serves as a set of rules and values that all company members must follow. Structural safety work requires employees to make decisions that impact clients, partners, and the general public.

Purpose of the Ethical Code

  • To clarify expectations: All staff members need to understand their specific work responsibilities.
  • To support responsible decision-making: The code serves as a reference point for employees to make ethical choices during challenging situations.
  • To protect clients and the public: NEURA-STRUCT operates with honesty and accuracy because its work affects structural safety.
  • To strengthen the company culture: Ethical conduct becomes an integral part of our shared values.

Professional and Technical Conduct

Accuracy in Work

Reports derive information from confirmed data sources. All measurements undergo verification tests before submission. All technical boundaries receive explicit documentation.

Avoiding Misrepresentation

Staff members stay away from all forms of deceptive practices including system performance exaggeration, hiding risks and uncertainties, and presenting false information.

Competence

Staff members should only take on work assignments that match their trained capabilities. Those lacking necessary skills should request assistance or training.

Ethical Behavior in the Workplace

Respect and Fair Treatment

NEURA-STRUCT maintains a policy prohibiting any form of discrimination based on gender, religion, nationality, age, disability, or personal characteristics.

Harassment and Bullying

The organization maintains a complete ban on all forms of harassment including physical, verbal, and psychological harassment. Staff members who experience harassment should contact their manager or the Ethics Committee.

Professional Attitude

Staff members maintain professionalism through being on time, meeting deadlines, maintaining clear communication, and teamwork.

Safety and Field Operations

All staff members must follow essential safety protocols:

  • All personnel must use assigned protective gear at work
  • All personnel must follow established entry protocols for site access
  • All personnel need to follow all posted warning signs about potential hazards
  • All personnel need to finish their required safety training program

Staff members who observe dangerous situations must report them immediately to their supervisors.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

NEURA-STRUCT maintains protected information about buildings and infrastructure systems. All staff members need to protect:

  • Structural data and vulnerability reports
  • Client information
  • Internal documents

Staff members must verify that all data entries remain accurate and complete while ensuring proper storage methods are followed. Any attempt to modify, remove, or create false data entries constitutes a severe ethical infraction.

Conduct Toward Clients and Partners

Honesty in Reporting

Technical reports must be complete, include all limitations, avoid any form of exaggeration, and show actual observations.

Equal Treatment

The company provides equal treatment to all clients regardless of project size.

Conflicts of Interest

Company employees must avoid situations creating conflicts between personal interests and business choices, including accepting gifts, working privately for clients, or recommending suppliers for personal gain.

Environmental Responsibility

NEURA-STRUCT operates with a mission to decrease its environmental footprint. The staff performs the following tasks for environmental protection:

  • Reduce waste production during installation operations
  • Recycle all materials which have the potential for reuse
  • Use tools that operate with maximum energy efficiency
  • Prevent site contamination during fieldwork activities

Anti-Corruption and Ethical Financial Conduct

Zero Tolerance for Corruption: The organization prohibits all forms of bribery, financial manipulation, and kickback activities.

Gifts and Hospitality: Staff members should decline all presents which could impact their professional choices or create the impression of influence.

Financial Transparency: All financial documents including budgets and invoices need to contain accurate and honest information.

Whistleblowing and Reporting

Staff members who want to report unethical conduct can contact their manager, Human Resources, or the Ethics Committee through confidential channels. The company protects all staff members from any form of punishment when they report genuine concerns.

Institutionalization of Ethics

NEURA-STRUCT implements ethics through established duties, ongoing communication, training programs, reporting systems, and monitoring tools. We aim to transform ethics into a fundamental operational practice.

The Ethics Committee

An internal committee that monitors ethical matters, answers ethics-related questions, reviews cases of misconduct, maintains and updates the Ethical Code, and provides ethical guidance to managers.

The Compliance Officer

Maintains the company's adherence to all applicable laws, industry standards, and internal policies. Performs internal inspections, verifies safety protocols, checks data protection methods, and assists staff with ethical rule comprehension.

Department Managers

Present the Ethical Code to team members, observe employee conduct during work hours, follow all safety protocols, maintain quality standards, and notify the committee about any ethical issues.

Lifelong Learning and Continuous Training

The engineering sector experiences rapid changes in technological advancements, safety regulations, and professional standards. NEURA-STRUCT recognizes lifelong learning as essential for survival and long-term success.

We maintain employee competence through ongoing training programs including:

  • Technical Training: Sensor installation, structural monitoring, data interpretation, and engineering standard updates
  • Ethical Training: Ethical dilemmas, conflicts of interest, anti-corruption rules, and data confidentiality practices
  • Safety Training: Field safety protocols, emergency response procedures, protective equipment usage, and hazard detection methods
  • Professional Development: Teamwork, conflict resolution, and client communication skills

Conclusion

The implementation of ethical practices at NEURA-STRUCT Engineering Technologies demonstrates that business ethics is not merely a theoretical concept, but a functional element that supports daily company operations and ensures long-term success.

Ethics as Foundation

For an engineering company working with structures and public safety, ethical responsibilities are paramount. The stability of buildings and public safety depend on responsible decisions, as any mistake can create immediate risks. This requires definite values, responsible conduct, and open communication channels.

From Code to Culture

The Ethical Code provides specific behavioral instructions for everyday work activities, but ethics requires active implementation to achieve its intended goals. Through official positions, communication systems, reporting mechanisms, performance audits, and disciplinary measures, NEURA-STRUCT integrates ethical conduct into its operational procedures.

Continuous Improvement

Ongoing education stands as a fundamental element for achieving both ethical and technical excellence. As new technologies and safety protocols emerge, the company's ethical standards improve through employee training, enabling NEURA-STRUCT to maintain its position as a trustworthy organization that delivers innovative solutions with responsibility.
